<h1>Notes</h1>
	By this point, your class build is starting to become more differentiated than simply using whatever you can find. This, unfortunately, is the ranged build at its weakest. Your two base options are <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Gold_armor">Gold armor</a> or <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Platinum_armor">Platinum armor</a>, but you may feel compelled to craft <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Shadow_armor">Shadow armor</a> for the movement speed. If you're having issues, don't be afraid to stack up on buffs and potions.
