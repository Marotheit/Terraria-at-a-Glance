<br/><br/><br/><h2>Notes</h2><div id="notes">In the early game, your first priority should be <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Cactus_armor">Cactus armor</a> (or <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Wood_armor">Wood armor</a> if you are unable to aquire enough <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Cactus">Cactus</a>,) and the <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Wooden_Bow">Wooden Bow</a> to make early fights as easy as possible. As you approach your first boss, you'll want to upgrade to <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Gold_armor">Gold armor</a> or <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Platinum_armor">Platinum armor</a> for the extra defense.</div>
