<h1>Notes</h1>
	When you first begin your journey, anything and everything you pick up will do, but your first priority should be <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Cactus_armor">Cactus armor</a> (or <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Pumpkin_armor">Pumpkin armor</a> if time allows) and a weapon. Ranged weapons will almost always perform better this early.<br/><br/>As you approach your first boss, <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/King_Slime">King Slime</a>, you'll want to begin moving towards the Pre-Skeletron build (even if you haven't quite defeated a boss yet) as many of the items can be acquired before fighting any bosses.
