<div id="notes"><h1>Notes</h1>
	<b>Notice: The Healer build is nearly identical to the Magic build until you reach Pre-Golem. Until that point, this guide will point you in the same direction as the Magic build.</b><br><br>When you first begin your journey, anything and everything you pick up will do, but your first priority should be <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Cactus_armor">Cactus armor</a> (or <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Wood_armor">Wood armor</a> if you are unable to acquire enough <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Cactus">Cactus</a>) and the <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Wooden_Bow">Wooden Bow</a> to make early fights as easy as possible. Ranged weapons will almost always perform better this early. As you approach your first boss, <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/King_Slime">King Slime</a>, you'll want to begin moving towards the Pre-Skeletron build (even if you haven't quite defeated a boss yet) as many of the items can be aquired before fighting any bosses.
</div>
