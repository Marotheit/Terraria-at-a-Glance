<h1>Notes<div id="tooltip"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Marotheit/Terraria-at-a-Glance/master/images/tooltip.png"><span id="tooltip_text">The Throwing build is not viable in Hardmode. After that point, this guide will be similar (if not identical) to the Ranged build.</span></div></h1>
	By this point, your class build is starting to become more differentiated than simply using whatever you can find. While you can survive with <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Gold_armor">Gold armor</a> or <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Platinum_armor">Platinum armor</a>, you'll find that <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Fossil_armor">Fossil armor</a> is far more useful when relying on throwing weapons.
