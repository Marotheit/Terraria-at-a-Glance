<h1>Notes</h1>
	By this point, your class build is starting to become more differentiated than simply using whatever you can find. While <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Gold_armor">Gold armor</a> or <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Platinum_armor">Platinum armor</a> will get you through your next few bosses, <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Crimson_armor">Crimson armor</a> or <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Shadow_armor">Shadow armor</a> will provide you with some very nice set bonuses that will allow you some extra survivability. For extra damage, do your best to track down <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Feral_Claws">Feral Claws</a> for the added attack speed.
