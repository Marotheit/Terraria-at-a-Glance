<h1>Notes<div id="tooltip"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Marotheit/Terraria-at-a-Glance/master/images/tooltip.png"><span id="tooltip_text">Because the Summoning build relies on companions, your weapon specialization is entirely up to you. Be sure to check other builds at this progression level to see which one seems the most enjoyable as a secondary class.</span></div></h1>
	Your first priority should be acquiring <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Cactus_armor">Cactus armor</a> (or <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Pumpkin_armor">Pumpkin armor</a> if time allows) and a weapon. Remember, just because you're a magic user doesn't mean the importance of a solid weapon can be understated this early in the game. If you come across an <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Enchanted_Boomerang">Enchanted Boomerang</a> or feel the need to create a <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Wooden_Bow">Wooden Bow</a>, there's no problem in doing so, since this early in the game you won't be using damage specific armor sets. As you approach your first boss, <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/King_Slime">King Slime</a>, you'll want to begin moving towards the Pre-Skeletron build (even if you haven't quite defeated a boss yet) as many of the items can be acquired before fighting any bosses.
