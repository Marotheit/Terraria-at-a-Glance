<h1>Notes<div id="tooltip"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Marotheit/Terraria-at-a-Glance/master/images/tooltip.png"><span id="tooltip_text">Because the Summoning build relies on companions, your weapon specialization is entirely up to you. 	Your first priority should be acquiring <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Cactus_armor">Cactus armor</a> (or <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Pumpkin_armor">Pumpkin armor</a> if time allows) and a weapon. As a summoner, your weapon specilization is up to you. Be sure to check other builds at this progression level to see which one seems the most enjoyable.<br/><br/>As you approach your first boss, <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/King_Slime">King Slime</a>, you'll want to begin moving towards the Pre-Skeletron build (even if you haven't quite defeated a boss yet) as many of the items can be acquired before fighting any bosses.
