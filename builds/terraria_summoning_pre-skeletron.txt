<h1>Notes<div id="tooltip"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Marotheit/Terraria-at-a-Glance/master/images/tooltip.png"><span id="tooltip_text">Because the Summoning build relies on companions, your weapon specialization is entirely up to you. Be sure to check other builds at this progression level to see which one seems the most enjoyable as a secondary class.</span></div></h1>
	By this point, your class build is starting to become more differentiated than simply using whatever you can find. While <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Gold_armor">Gold armor</a> or <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Platinum_armor">Platinum armor</a> will get you through your next few bosses, <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Bee_armor">Bee armor</a> will provide you with some extra damage to make boss fights easier. If you're having issues, don't be afraid to swap to <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Crimson_armor">Crimson armor</a> or <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Shadow_armor">Shadow armor</a> for the extra survivability.
