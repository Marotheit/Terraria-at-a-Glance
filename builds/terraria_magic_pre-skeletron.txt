<h1>Notes</h1>
	By this point, your class build is starting to become more differentiated than simply using whatever you can find. While <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Gold_armor">Gold armor</a> or <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Platinum_armor">Platinum armor</a> will get you through your next few bosses, <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Meteor_armor">Meteor armor</a> or <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Jungle_armor">Jungle armor</a> will provide you with some very nice set bonuses that will allow you to kill enemies faster and more efficiently. Do your best to get yourself a <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Demon_Scythe">Demon Scythe</a> if the opportunity arises, as this could be your primary weapon for some time, even into early Hardmode.
