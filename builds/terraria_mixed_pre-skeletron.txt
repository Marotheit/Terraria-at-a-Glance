<h1>Notes</h1>
	By this point, your class build is starting to become more differentiated than simply using whatever you can find. While <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Gold_armor">Gold armor</a> or <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Platinum_armor">Platinum armor</a> will get you through your next few bosses, <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Crimson_armor">Crimson armor</a> or <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Shadow_armor">Shadow armor</a> is the obvious next step in progression.
