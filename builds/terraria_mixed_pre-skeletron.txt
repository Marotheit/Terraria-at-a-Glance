<div id="notes"><h1>Notes</h1>
	By this point, you should be nearing your first boss encounter, <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/King_Slime">King Slime</a>. Following this, the <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Eye_of_Cthulhu">Eye of Cthulhu</a> will follow soon after. Both bosses are not particularly difficult as long as you keep moving and have a ranged weapon that can be used to kite them. Be sure to use your buffs and potions and you should be just fine. Both <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Eater_of_Worlds">Eater of Worlds</a> (for Corruption worlds) and <a href="https://terraria.gamepedia.com/Brain_of_Cthulhu">Brain of Cthulhu</a> (for Crimson worlds) are gear checks more than anything else. Just be sure not to trap yourself in your fighting areas!
